About Us

Ratna Solomon College of Elementary Education

Ratna Solomon College of Elementary Education is a dedicated institution committed to preparing skilled and responsible teachers for primary education. The college is organised and managed by Hanna Charitable Trust, Rampachodavaram, with the objective of promoting quality teacher education and strengthening the foundation of elementary schooling.

The institution offers the Diploma in Elementary Education (D.Ed) programme, which is designed to equip future teachers with the knowledge, teaching skills, and professional values required to guide young learners effectively. The programme follows a structured academic framework combining theoretical learning with practical teaching experience to ensure well-rounded teacher training.

The D.Ed course has a duration of 2 years, allowing students to develop strong pedagogical abilities, classroom management skills, and an understanding of child development. The college maintains a focused learning environment with 2 units and an approved intake strength of 100 students, ensuring proper academic attention and training for every trainee.

Ratna Solomon College of Elementary Education emphasizes academic discipline, professional ethics, and community service. Through experienced faculty, supportive infrastructure, and a learner-centric approach, the institution aims to produce competent and committed elementary teachers who can contribute meaningfully to the education system and society.

The college continues to work towards its mission of nurturing dedicated educators who can build a strong educational foundation for future generations.
